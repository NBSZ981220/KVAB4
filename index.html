About us

Our common story started in 2018. At the beginning we wrote our own songs, first for each other and then we played music in a group of friends. In September 2019 we had our first concert, so we don’t have much experience, but we say funnily: „We're getting off the wall guard now, but we're trying to get off very quickly”. Our first two albums have already been appeared, one of them consist of Hungarian songs, and the other one contains only English songs. Neither we know the genre exactly, therefore we always say that it is indie or alternative, maybe the people don’t ask about it anymore …. and it is cool anyway :>
Our repertoire essentially consist of our own songs and poem processing, this is complemented by processing of other bands songs.
After the first concert we played in a few small pubs; we had live radio concert in Marosvásárhelyi Radio, in the „Sheet music” entitled broadcast; we participated in the Targu Mures phase of the Peron Music Talent Show, where we took the first place; we performed/played in the „Stay at Home!” Transilvanian festival, and we celebrated the Hungarian Poetry Day with online poem concert.
